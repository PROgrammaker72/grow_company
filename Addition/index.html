Start with a free home assessment. This will give everyone a better idea of what the scope of the project will be and what type of drawings and services will be required. Once the home assessment is done and we have taken all the necessary measurements (this may require more than one visit to your home), we will prepare a set of drawings for a permit application. This set includes a site plan, floor plans, elevations and details as required by the scope of your project.

Then we would cooperate with our contractors to provide a full set of construction drawings for their use and manage the building process.
