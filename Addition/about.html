Designing and Building Your Vision

About Us

From the inception of a project, to creating the design, to getting the necessary permits, and finally, to construction, Acadia is there every step of the way. We offer our clients the inspiration and support they need to create something that is truly unique. At Acadia, we meet with every client in person to discuss exactly what they hope to achieve with a project. With our client’s vision in mind, we use the latest in computer-assisted design and drafting software to put ideas onto paper, and finally, into reality.

Our mission

Beautiful spaces are all around us.

If you’ve ever stopped to wonder what makes a beautiful space so inviting, you’ve considered the main question at the core of everything we do at Acadia. We are constantly striving to distill what it is that makes beautiful, functional, exciting, inviting, invigorating spaces so universally appealing. All of us have spent time in buildings that are sometimes less than spectacular - that is why Acadia’s goal is to help businesses and individuals create the ideal space for their purpose. Be it for meetings, shopping, leisure, or even warehousing, there is always a way that our team can provide inspiration, encouragement, assistance or direction.

What We Do

Consultation

Design & Drafting

Construction
