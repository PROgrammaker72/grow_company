Pick 6ix Restaurant

33 Yonge Street, Toronto

Drake’s latest addition to Toronto’s nightlife is bound to be the hottest spot in the city. Pick 6 is currently under construction and is due to open early 2018. Acadia was instrumental in designing the space, drafting, obtaining permits and overseeing construction on all fronts. Currently, work is underway to create a stunning interior for the much anticipated restaurant’s debut.

Pick 6ix is preparing for their grand opening! Conveniently located steps from Union or King Stations and of course, the Financial District as well as the Air Canada Centre.

This upscale venue will open soon thanks to an incredible amount of hard work by a number of professionals and firms of which Acadia Design Consultants Inc. has contributed, largely in part, with the preparation of the architectural design, in obtaining the necessary building permits and management of the constructions process as a General Contractor. This collaborative work has been widely featured on a number of online news media due to the private pre-opening.
